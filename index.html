Who We Are

Empowering Communities, Creating Change

The Suhail Foundation is dedicated to empowering children with genetic conditions, particularly Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy (DMD). Our mission is to support these exceptional children on their journey toward independence, offering assistance with accessibility, specialized equipment, education, physical therapy, and social support.

Established in memory of our son, Suhail, who passed away at 24 in 2023 after living a remarkable life in the face of the challenges posed by DMD, the foundation aims to perpetuate his courageous spirit and remarkable accomplishments. Our goal is to help others reach their full potential, no matter their limitations.

We aspire to create a movement founded on hope and positivity, reaching out to like-minded donors as our first step. At the Suhail Foundation, we firmly believe that, with the right resources and support, every child can lead a full and fulfilling life, and we are committed to serving as a source of assistance and opportunity for those facing the challenges of genetic conditions.
